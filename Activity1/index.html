SPIDER

Kawasaki Ninja Spider

A conceptual monochrome take on the iconic Kawasaki Ninja — the "Spider" edition blends aggressive lines with a stripped-back black & white aesthetic.

The Ninja Spider is imagined as a lightweight sport machine with razor-sharp handling and a minimal visual language. This page is a simple, high-contrast showcase focusing on typography, layout, and essential specs for clarity and attitude.

Engine
Inline-4, 998cc
High-revving sport tuned
Power
200 hp
Peak power, lightweight chassis
Weight
180 kg
Dry weight target

Design

The Spider's design reduces distraction: surfaces are either deep matte black or bright white highlights. Its silhouette is aggressive with a tucked riding position, a sharply raked tail, and minimalist lighting elements. The contrast-driven palette emphasises form and detail without relying on colour.

Performance

With a high-output engine and a chassis focused on agility, the Spider prioritises corner speed and rider feedback. Electronics are present but pared down to essentials: traction control, quickshifter, and selectable engine maps to suit both street and track use.
